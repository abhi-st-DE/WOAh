This "PostMan" application is a personal, non-commercial tool developed by Abhishek Choudhary for the sole purpose of automating content sharing to the developer's own LinkedIn profile via the official LinkedIn API.

In compliance with LinkedIn's platform terms, this application does not store member data or social activity data for more than 24 hours. No permanent database of your activity is maintained.

4. User Control & Revocation

You can revoke this application's access at any time through your LinkedIn Permitted Services settings.
